## 1. Nota de R$50 / R$50 Bill 💵

**Time:** 0s–10s
**Environment PT:** Caixa registradora de supermercado, luz fria de néon, prateleiras lotadas ao fundo
**Environment EN:** Supermarket cash register, cold neon light, crowded shelves in background
**Error PT:** Gastar o dinheiro do mês inteiro em compras por impulso no supermercado
**Error EN:** Spending the entire monthly budget on impulse purchases at the supermarket
**Expression arc:** angustiada → indignada → desesperada

### Midjourney Prompt
```
Anthropomorphic R$50 Brazilian banknote character, Pixar 3D render style, big expressive teary eyes, small trembling hands, dramatic lighting with cold neon supermarket glow, 9:16 vertical format, shallow depth of field, blurred background showing a Brazilian person loading a shopping cart with random items, emotional facial expression shifting from hopeful to devastated, ultra-detailed paper texture, green and purple color tones of real R$50 bill, cinematic composition, Disney-Pixar quality rendering --ar 9:16 --v 6
```

### Kling/Runway Prompt
```
Animate a 3D Pixar-style R$50 Brazilian banknote character standing at a supermarket checkout. Begin with arms crossed and a proud posture, then slowly slump as a shopping cart full of random items passes by. Eyes widen in horror, hands reach out dramatically as if trying to stop the transaction. Body shakes with exaggerated sorrow. Camera slowly zooms into the character's face for a close-up of tearful despair. Smooth, expressive Disney-quality animation, 10 seconds total.
```

## 3. Boleto / Bill/Invoice 📄

**Time:** 20s–30s
**Environment PT:** Aplicativo de banco no celular, fundo escuro e dramático, código de barras pulsando como um coração ameaçador

**Error PT:** Deixar boletos vencerem por esquecimento e pagar multa e juros desnecessários
**Error EN:** Letting bills expire from forgetfulness and paying unnecessary fines and interest
**Expression arc:** ameaçador → implacável → triunfante

```

```
Animate a 3D Pixar-style Brazilian boleto/bill character standing tall and intimidating in a dark environment. Character begins with arms crossed and a slow, deliberate head turn toward camera.
