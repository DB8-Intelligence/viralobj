**Hook:** Your plants are trying to tell you something… and you're not listening 🌿

🌸 Desert Rose — Less water, not more. It stores water in its roots. Overwatering = rotting from the inside.

🪴 Pothos — It LOVES shade. Direct sun yellows and scorches the leaves. Move it NOW.

🌹 Rose — Water on leaves invites fungus. Always, ALWAYS water at the base.

Care with intelligence, not just with love. 💚
